## Meeting Hippareones

[Gary](../../directory/Deverain/Gary.md), [Pebble](../../directory/Sigisfarne/Pebble.md), [Oswin](../../directory/Sigisfarne/Oswin.md) and [Ayrrin](../../directory/Sigisfarne/Ayrrin.md) return to the [Underground Temple](../../directory/FoldedBelow/UndergroundTemple.md).
In the church below, they see four [Hippareiones](../../directory/FoldedBelow/Hippareiones.md), while they carry a statue of the sister of the [Weeping Mother](../../directory/weepingMother/index.md).
Two of them are hurt.

The group befriended them and agreed to pay for their access in the [Yellow Smoke](../../directory/FoldedBelow/YellowSmoke.md), in exchange for their help to get more of the [yellow powder](ep_026.md).

Inside the [Yellow Smoke](../../directory/FoldedBelow/YellowSmoke.md) den they went through the bar and arrived in a back room.
It smelled like excrement.

## Drug brewing

Under a lot of drug smoke from the den, the Hippareiones brokered a deal with the bouncers.
The four villagers were allowed into the drug brewing chamber.
They say the "helpers" will give them some stolen yellow powder.

Upon entering the chamber, they realise the "helpers" are enslaved villagers from the region.
The chamber is a balcony, overlooking a cliff.
The cliff is one of the multiple mesas in the deadlands to the northwest.
Far in the horizon, the [Wall](../../directory/Wall/index.md) can be seen to the left and the [Titan Spire](../../directory/DuskmeadowFringe/SkySpire.md) to the right.

The alchemist slaves explain that they receive poor instructions and are forced to brew and test the potent drugs.
When asked about their villages, they say that they have been decimated by the Hippareones.

## Allucinations

Pebble decides to use the leftover yellow powder from his brother [Stone](../../directory/Sigisfarne/Stone.md).
He sees southern coins that bite, all over the floor.
The drug smoke puffs.

Gary decides to also take some powder.
In his vision, he sees the Stone Titan fighting giants that crossed the wall.
The [same vision he had before](ep_029.md), but this time seeing from atop the deadlands mesas.

The group then tries to smuggle the slaves out of this madness.
They are caught on their way out and escorted.
It is all confusion, but once out of the den, the hippareones and the spider mercenaries attack and kill everyone in the group.

*But was that just another bad trip?*
